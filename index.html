The Pali Tipitika and Translations.

The authors of Sutta Central have opened the ancient pali writings for everyone. They have shared Lord Buddha's gift of Dhamma so that all of us can gain greater understanding of the profound wisdom of the Tathagatha.

Theravadan.org is a representation of their work.

Their translations are presented here in small web-pages that hopefully will reveal their work in Dhamma.

Vinaya

Usually translated as "The Discipline", the Vinaya describes the code of conduct of the monastics (Sangha) who have dedicated their lives to the Buddha, Dhamma and Sangha.

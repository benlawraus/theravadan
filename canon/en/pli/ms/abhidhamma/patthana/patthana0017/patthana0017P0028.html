Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhammānulomapaccanīya (3), Tikatikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (5)

17.28. Sanidassanattika, Vedanāttika

Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho nasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi …pe… avigate cha.

Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ dukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho nadukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi …pe… vipāke tīṇi …pe… avigate cha.

Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho naadukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi …pe… avigate cha.

17.4. Kusalattika, Saṅkiliṭṭhattika

Akusalaṁ saṅkiliṭṭhasaṅkilesikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naakusalo nasaṅkiliṭṭhasaṅkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi …pe… avigate tīṇi.

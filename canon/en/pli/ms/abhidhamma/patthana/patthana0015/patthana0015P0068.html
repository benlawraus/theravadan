Dhammānulomapaccanīya (3), Dukatikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (3)

15.68. Niyataduka, Kusalattika

Niyataṁ kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naniyato nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Aniyataṁ kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naniyato nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve …pe… avigate dve.

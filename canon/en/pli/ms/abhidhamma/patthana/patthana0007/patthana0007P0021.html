Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhammapaccanīya (2), Tikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (1)

7.21. Ajjhattārammaṇattika

Naajjhattārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naajjhattārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nabahiddhārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

Nabahiddhārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nabahiddhārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nabahiddhārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

Hetuyā nava …pe… avigate nava.

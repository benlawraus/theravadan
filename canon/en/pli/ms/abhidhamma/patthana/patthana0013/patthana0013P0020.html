Dhammānulomapaccanīya (3), Tikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (1)

13.20. Ajjhattattika

Ajjhattaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nabahiddhā dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

Bahiddhā dhammaṁ paṭicca naajjhatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhammānulomapaccanīya (3), Tikadukapaṭṭhānapāḷi (4)

16.49. Sanidassanattika, Hetusahetukaduka

Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ hetuñceva sahetukañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nahetu ceva naahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi …pe… avigate tīṇi.

Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ sahetukañceva na ca hetuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naahetuko ceva nanahetu ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi …pe… avigate tīṇi.

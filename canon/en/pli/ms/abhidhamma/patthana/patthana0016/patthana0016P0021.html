16.21. Kusalattika, Cittasahabhūduka

Kusalaṁ cittasahabhuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo nacittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … pañca.

Akusalaṁ cittasahabhuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naakusalo nacittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … pañca.

Abyākataṁ cittasahabhuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo nacittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

Hetuyā terasa, ārammaṇe nava …pe… vipāke tīṇi …pe… avigate terasa.

Abyākataṁ nocittasahabhuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo nanocittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhammapaccanīyānuloma (4), Tikatikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (5)

23.29. Sekkhattika, Kusalattika

Nasekkhaṁ nakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā sekkho kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha …pe… avigate cha.

Nasekkhaṁ naakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā nevasekkhanāsekkho akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi …pe… avigate tīṇi.

Nasekkhaṁ naabyākataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nevasekkhanāsekkho abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca …pe… avigate pañca.

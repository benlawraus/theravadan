Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhammapaccanīyānuloma (4), Tikatikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (5)

23.49. Sanidassanattika, Sekkhattika

Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nasekkhaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho sekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi …pe… avigate tīṇi.

Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ naasekkhaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho asekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi …pe… avigate tīṇi.

Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nanevasekkhanāsekkhaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa …pe… avigate ekavīsa.

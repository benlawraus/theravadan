Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhammapaccanīyānuloma (4), Tikatikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (5)

23.24. Vitakkattika, Kusalattika

Nasavitakkasavicāraṁ nakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā savitakkasavicāro kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā pannarasa …pe… avigate pannarasa.

Nasavitakkasavicāraṁ naakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā savitakkasavicāro akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā nava …pe… avigate nava.

Nasavitakkasavicāraṁ naabyākataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca avitakkaavicāro abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā satta, adhipatiyā satta …pe… avigate satta.

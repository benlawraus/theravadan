Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhammapaccanīyānuloma (4), Dukatikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (3)

21.38. Sahetukadukādi, Sanidassanattika

Nasahetukaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca ahetuko sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṁ.

Naahetukaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca ahetuko sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṁ. (Gaṇitakena tīṇi pañhā. Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi …pe… avigate tīṇi.

(Nasahetukanaanidassanasappaṭighamūlepi tīṇiyeva.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi …pe… aññamaññe ekaṁ …pe… avigate tīṇi.

Nasahetukaṁ naanidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca ahetuko anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekaṁ, adhipatiyā ekaṁ …pe… avigate ekaṁ.

(Nahetusampayuttaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ sahetukadukasadisaṁ. Tīṇi pañhā. Hetusahetukaduke ca hetuhetusampayuttaduke ca pañhā no labbhati.)

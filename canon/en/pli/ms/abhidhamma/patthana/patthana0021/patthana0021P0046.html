21.46. Āsavaduka, Sanidassanattika

Naāsavaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca noāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṁ.

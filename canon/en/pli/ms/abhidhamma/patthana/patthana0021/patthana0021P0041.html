Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhammapaccanīyānuloma (4), Dukatikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (3)

21.41. Sanidassanaduka, Sanidassanattika

Nasanidassanaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassano sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekaṁ, adhipatiyā ekaṁ …pe… avigate ekaṁ.

Nasanidassanaṁ naanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca anidassano anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekaṁ, adhipatiyā ekaṁ …pe… avigate ekaṁ.

Nasanidassanaṁ naanidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca anidassano anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekaṁ, adhipatiyā ekaṁ …pe… avigate ekaṁ.

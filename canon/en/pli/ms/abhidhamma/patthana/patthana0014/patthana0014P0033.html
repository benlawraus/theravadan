Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhammānulomapaccanīya (3), Dukapaṭṭhānapāḷi (2)

14.33. Pariyāpannaduka

Pariyāpannaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naapariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

Apariyāpannaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naapariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Apariyāpannaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca napariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Apariyāpannaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca napariyāpanno ca naapariyāpanno ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā.

Pariyāpannañca apariyāpannañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naapariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve …pe… avigate pañca.

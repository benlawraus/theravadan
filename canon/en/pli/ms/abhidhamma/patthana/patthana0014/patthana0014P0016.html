Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhammānulomapaccanīya (3), Dukapaṭṭhānapāḷi (2)

14.16. Āsavasāsavaduka

Āsavañceva sāsavañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavo ceva naanāsavo ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Āsavañceva sāsavañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naanāsavo ceva nano ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Āsavañceva sāsavañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavo ceva naanāsavo ca naanāsavo ceva nano ca āsavo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā.

Sāsavañceva no ca āsavaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanāsavo ceva nano ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

Āsavañceva sāsavañca sāsavañceva no ca āsavañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavo ceva naanāsavo ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

Hetuyā nava …pe… vipāke ekaṁ …pe… avigate nava. (Sabbattha nava.)

Saccapāramī 2
The Perfection of Truth (2nd)

Saccatāpasacariya
The Ascetic Truthful’s Conduct

“Punāparaṁ yadā homi,
“Then again when I was

Tāpaso saccasavhayo;
an ascetic known as Truthful,

Saccena lokaṁ pālesiṁ,
I protected the world with my truth,

Samaggaṁ janamakāsahan”ti.
uniting the people.”

Saccatāpasacariyaṁ aṭṭhamaṁ.

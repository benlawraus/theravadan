Cariyāpiṭaka The Conduct Leading to Buddhahood

Akittivagga The Chapter With Akitti

Dānapāramī 5 The Perfection of Giving (5th)

Mahāgovindacariya Mahāgovinda’s Conduct

“Punāparaṁ yadā homi, “Then again when I was

sattarājapurohito; high priest for seven kings,

Pūjito naradevehi, I was venerated by the gods among men

mahāgovindabrāhmaṇo. as the brahmin Mahāgovinda.

Tadāhaṁ sattarajjesu, Then in all seven kingdoms

Yaṁ me āsi upāyanaṁ; what I receieved as tribute

Tena demi mahādānaṁ, I used to give a great gift

Akkhobbhaṁ sāgarūpamaṁ. unwavering as the ocean.

Na me dessaṁ dhanaṁ dhaññaṁ, It was not because I disliked riches or grain,

Napi natthi nicayo mayi; or because I had a store set aside,

Sabbaññutaṁ piyaṁ mayhaṁ, but because omniscience is precious to me,

Tasmā demi varaṁ dhanan”ti. that is why I gave such fine riches.”

Mahāgovindacariyaṁ pañcamaṁ.

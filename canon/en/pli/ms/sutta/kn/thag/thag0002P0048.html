Theragāthā 2.48 Verses of the Senior Monks 2.48

Dukanipāta The Book of the Twos

Pañcamavagga Chapter Five

Vajjitattheragāthā Vajjita

“Saṁsaraṁ dīghamaddhānaṁ, Transmigrating for such a long time,

gatīsu parivattisaṁ; I’ve proceeded through various states of rebirth,

Apassaṁ ariyasaccāni, not seeing the noble truths,

andhabhūto puthujjano. a blind, unenlightened person.

Tassa me appamattassa, But when I became heedful,

saṁsārā vinaḷīkatā; transmigrations were mown down.

Sabbā gatī samucchinnā, All states of rebirth are cut off;

natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti. now there’ll be no more future lives.

… Vajjito thero ….

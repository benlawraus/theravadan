Theragāthā 1.47 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.47

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Pañcamavagga Chapter Five

Ujjayattheragāthā Ujjaya

“Namo te buddha vīratthu, Homage to you, O Buddha, O hero,

vippamuttosi sabbadhi; freed in every way!

Tuyhāpadāne viharaṁ, Meditating in the fruits of your practice,

viharāmi anāsavo”ti. I live without defilements.

… Ujjayo thero ….

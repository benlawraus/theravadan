Theragāthā 1.66 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.66

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Sattamavagga Chapter Seven

Meghiyattheragāthā Meghiya

“Anusāsi mahāvīro, He counseled me, the great hero,

sabbadhammāna pāragū; the one who has gone beyond all things.

Tassāhaṁ dhammaṁ sutvāna, When I heard his teaching

vihāsiṁ santike sato; I stayed close by him, mindful.

Tisso vijjā anuppattā, I’ve attained the three knowledges

kataṁ buddhassa sāsanan”ti. and fulfilled the Buddha’s instructions.

… Meghiyo thero ….

Theragāthā 1.99 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.99

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Dasamavagga Chapter Ten

Uttiyattheragāthā Uttiya (3rd)

“Saddaṁ sutvā sati muṭṭhā, When you hear a sound, mindfulness is lost

Piyaṁ nimittaṁ manasikaroto; as you focus on a pleasant feature.

Sārattacitto vedeti, Experiencing it with a mind full of desire,

Tañca ajjhosa tiṭṭhati; you keep clinging to it.

Tassa vaḍḍhanti āsavā, Your defilements grow,

Saṁsāraṁ upagāmino”ti. leading to transmigration.

… Uttiyo thero ….

Theragāthā 1.48
Verses of the Senior Monks 1.48

Ekakanipāta
The Book of the Ones

Pañcamavagga
Chapter Five

Sañjayattheragāthā
Sañjaya

“Yato ahaṁ pabbajito,
Since I went forth

Agārasmānagāriyaṁ;
from the lay life to homelessness,

Nābhijānāmi saṅkappaṁ,
I’ve not been aware of any thought

Anariyaṁ dosasaṁhitan”ti.
that is ignoble and hateful.

… Sañjayo thero ….

Theragāthā 1.74 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.74

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Aṭṭhamavagga Chapter Eight

Suyāmanattheragāthā Suyāmana

“Kāmacchando ca byāpādo, Sensual desire, ill will,

Thinamiddhañca bhikkhuno; dullness and drowsiness,

Uddhaccaṁ vicikicchā ca, restlessness, and doubt

Sabbasova na vijjatī”ti. are not found in a monk at all.

… Suyāmano thero ….

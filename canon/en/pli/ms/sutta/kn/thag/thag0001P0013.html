Theragāthā 1.13 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.13

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Dutiyavagga Chapter Two

Vanavacchattheragāthā Vanavaccha (1st)

“Nīlabbhavaṇṇā rucirā, Glistening, they look like blue storm clouds,

sītavārī sucindharā; with waters cool and streams so clear,

Indagopakasañchannā, and covered all in ladybugs:

te selā ramayanti man”ti. these rocky crags delight me!

… Vanavaccho thero ….

Theragāthā 1.51 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.51

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Chaṭṭhavagga Chapter Six

Godhikattheragāthā Godhika

“Vassati devo yathā sugītaṁ, The heavens rain like a sweet song;

Channā me kuṭikā sukhā nivātā; my little hut is roofed and pleasant, sheltered from the wind.

Cittaṁ susamāhitañca mayhaṁ, My mind is serene:

Atha ce patthayasi pavassa devā”ti. so rain forth, heavens, if you wish.

… Godhiko thero ….

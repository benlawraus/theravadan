Theragāthā 2.12 Verses of the Senior Monks 2.12

Dukanipāta The Book of the Twos

Dutiyavagga Chapter Two

Jotidāsattheragāthā Jotidāsa

“Ye kho te veṭhamissena, People who act harshly—

nānattena ca kammunā; attacking people,

Manusse uparundhanti, binding them with rope,

pharusūpakkamā janā; hurting them in all kinds of ways—

Tepi tattheva kīranti, they’re treated in the same way;

na hi kammaṁ panassati. their deeds don’t vanish.

Yaṁ karoti naro kammaṁ, Whatever deeds a person does,

Kalyāṇaṁ yadi pāpakaṁ; whether good or bad,

Tassa tasseva dāyādo, they are the heir to each

Yaṁ yaṁ kammaṁ pakubbatī”ti. and every deed they do.

… Jotidāso thero ….

Theragāthā 1.26
Verses of the Senior Monks 1.26

Ekakanipāta
The Book of the Ones

Tatiyavagga
Chapter Three

Abhayattheragāthā
Abhaya

“Sutvā subhāsitaṁ vācaṁ,
Having heard the fine words

buddhassādiccabandhuno;
of the Buddha, the kinsman of the Sun,

Paccabyadhiṁ hi nipuṇaṁ,
I penetrated the subtle truth,

vālaggaṁ usunā yathā”ti.
like a hair-tip with an arrow.

… Abhayo thero ….

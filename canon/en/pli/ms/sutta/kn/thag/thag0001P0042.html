Pañcamavagga
Chapter Five

Khadiravaniyattheragāthā
Revata of the Acacia Wood

“Cāle upacāle sīsūpacāle (…),
Cāla, Upacāla, and Sīsupacāla

Patissatā nu kho viharatha;
meditate mindfully!

Āgato vo vālaṁ viya vedhī”ti.
I’ve come to you like a hair-splitter.

… Khadiravaniyo thero ….

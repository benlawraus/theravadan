Theragāthā 2.33 Verses of the Senior Monks 2.33

Dukanipāta The Book of the Twos

Catutthavagga Chapter Four

Upavāṇattheragāthā Upavāna

“Arahaṁ sugato loke, The perfected one, the Holy One in the world,

vātehābādhiko muni; the sage is afflicted by winds.

Sace uṇhodakaṁ atthi, If there’s hot water,

munino dehi brāhmaṇa. give it to the sage, brahmin.

Pūjito pūjaneyyānaṁ, I wish to bring it to the one

sakkareyyāna sakkato; who is esteemed by the estimable,

Apacitopaceyyānaṁ, honored by the honorable,

tassa icchāmi hātave”ti. and venerated by the venerable.

… Upavāṇo thero ….

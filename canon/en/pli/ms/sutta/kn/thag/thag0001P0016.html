Theragāthā 1.16 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.16

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Dutiyavagga Chapter Two

Belaṭṭhasīsattheragāthā Belaṭṭhasīsa

“Yathāpi bhaddo ājañño, Just as a fine thoroughbred

naṅgalāvattanī sikhī; proceeds with ease,

Gacchati appakasirena, tail and mane flying in the wind;

evaṁ rattindivā mama; so my days and nights

Gacchanti appakasirena, proceed with ease,

sukhe laddhe nirāmise”ti. full of joy not of the flesh.

… Belaṭṭhasīso thero ….

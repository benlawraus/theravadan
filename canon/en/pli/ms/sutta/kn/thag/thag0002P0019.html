Theragāthā 2.19 Verses of the Senior Monks 2.19

Dukanipāta The Book of the Twos

Dutiyavagga Chapter Two

Nandattheragāthā Nanda

“Ayoniso manasikārā, Because of focusing on the wrong things,

maṇḍanaṁ anuyuñjisaṁ; I was addicted to ornamentation.

Uddhato capalo cāsiṁ, I was vain, fickle,

kāmarāgena aṭṭito. racked by desire for pleasures of the senses.

Upāyakusalenāhaṁ, But with the help of the Buddha,

buddhenādiccabandhunā; the kinsman of the Sun, so skilled in means,

Yoniso paṭipajjitvā, I practiced rationally and extracted

bhave cittaṁ udabbahin”ti. attachment to continued existence from my mind.

… Nando thero ….

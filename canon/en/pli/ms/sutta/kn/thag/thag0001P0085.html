Theragāthā 1.85 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.85

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Navamavagga Chapter Nine

Sunāgattheragāthā Sunāga

“Cittanimittassa kovido, Skilled in the patterns of the mind,

Pavivekarasaṁ vijāniya; understanding the nectar of seclusion,

Jhāyaṁ nipako patissato, practicing absorption, alert, mindful:

Adhigaccheyya sukhaṁ nirāmisan”ti. such a person would realize pleasure not of the flesh.

… Sunāgo thero ….

Theragāthā 2.47 Verses of the Senior Monks 2.47

Dukanipāta The Book of the Twos

Pañcamavagga Chapter Five

Anūpamattheragāthā Anūpama

“Nandamānāgataṁ cittaṁ, The conceited mind, addicted to pleasure,

sūlamāropamānakaṁ; impales itself on its own stake.

Tena teneva vajasi, It always goes where

yena sūlaṁ kaliṅgaraṁ. there’s a stake, a chopping board.

Tāhaṁ cittakaliṁ brūmi, I declare you the loser mind!

taṁ brūmi cittadubbhakaṁ; I declare you the insidious mind!

Satthā te dullabho laddho, You’ve found the teacher so hard to find—

mānatthe maṁ niyojayī”ti. don’t lead me away from the goal.

… Anūpamo thero ….

Theragāthā 1.14 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.14

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Dutiyavagga Chapter Two

Sivakasāmaṇeragāthā The Novice Sivaka

“Upajjhāyo maṁ avaca, My mentor said to me:

ito gacchāma sīvaka; “Let’s leave here, Sīvaka.”

Gāme me vasati kāyo, My body lives in the village,

araññaṁ me gato mano; but my mind has gone to the wilderness.

Semānakopi gacchāmi, I go there even when lying down—

natthi saṅgo vijānatan”ti. you can’t snare those who know.

… Vanavacchassa therassa (siviko) sāmaṇero ….

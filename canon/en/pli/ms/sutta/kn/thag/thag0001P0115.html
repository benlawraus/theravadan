Theragāthā 1.115 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.115

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Dvādasamavagga Chapter Twelve

Mahānāmattheragāthā Mahānāma

“Esāvahiyyase pabbatena, By Mount Nesādaka,

Bahukuṭajasallakikena; with its famous covering

Nesādakena girinā, of abundant shrubs and trees,

Yasassinā paricchadenā”ti. you’re found deficient.

… Mahānāmo thero ….

Theragāthā 1.79
Verses of the Senior Monks 1.79

Ekakanipāta
The Book of the Ones

Aṭṭhamavagga
Chapter Eight

Rakkhitattheragāthā
Rakkhita

“Sabbo rāgo pahīno me,
All my lust is given up,

sabbo doso samūhato;
all my hate is eradicated,

Sabbo me vigato moho,
all my delusion is gone:

sītibhūtosmi nibbuto”ti.
I’m cooled, quenched.

… Rakkhito thero ….

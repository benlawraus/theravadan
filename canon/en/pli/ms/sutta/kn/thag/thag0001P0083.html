Theragāthā 1.83
Verses of the Senior Monks 1.83

Ekakanipāta
The Book of the Ones

Navamavagga
Chapter Nine

Sīhattheragāthā
Sīha

“Sīhappamatto vihara,
Meditate diligently, Sīha,

rattindivamatandito;
tireless all day and night.

Bhāvehi kusalaṁ dhammaṁ,
Develop skillful qualities,

jaha sīghaṁ samussayan”ti.
and quickly discard this bag of bones.

… Sīho thero ….

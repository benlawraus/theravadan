Theragāthā 1.33 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.33

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Catutthavagga Chapter Four

Sopākattheragāthā Sopāka (1st)

“Yathāpi ekaputtasmiṁ, Just as a mother would be good

piyasmiṁ kusalī siyā; to her beloved only child;

Evaṁ sabbesu pāṇesu, so, to creatures all and everywhere,

sabbattha kusalo siyā”ti. let one be good.

… Sopāko thero ….

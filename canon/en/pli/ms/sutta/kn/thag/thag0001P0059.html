Theragāthā 1.59 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.59

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Chaṭṭhavagga Chapter Six

Kosalavihārittheragāthā Kosalavihārin

“Saddhāyāhaṁ pabbajito, I went forth out of faith

araññe me kuṭikā katā; and built a little hut in the wilderness.

Appamatto ca ātāpī, I’m heedful, ardent,

sampajāno patissato”ti. aware, and mindful.

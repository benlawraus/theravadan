Theragāthā 1.28 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.28

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Tatiyavagga Chapter Three

Jambugāmikaputtattheragāthā Jambugāmikaputta

“Kacci no vatthapasuto, Aren’t you obsessed with clothes?

Kacci no bhūsanārato; Don’t you just love jewelry?

Kacci sīlamayaṁ gandhaṁ, Is it not you—and no-one else—

Kiṁ tvaṁ vāyasi netarā pajā”ti. who spreads the scent of virtue?

… Jambugāmikaputto thero ….

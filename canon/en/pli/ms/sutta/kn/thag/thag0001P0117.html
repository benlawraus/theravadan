Theragāthā 1.117 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.117

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Dvādasamavagga Chapter Twelve

Yasattheragāthā Yasa

“Suvilitto suvasano, I’m well-anointed and well-dressed,

sabbābharaṇabhūsito; adorned with all my jewellery.

Tisso vijjā ajjhagamiṁ, I’ve attained the three knowledges

kataṁ buddhassa sāsanan”ti. and fulfilled the Buddha’s instructions.

… Yaso thero ….

Theragāthā 1.78 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.78

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Aṭṭhamavagga Chapter Eight

Meṇḍasirattheragāthā Meṇḍasira

“Anekajātisaṁsāraṁ, Transmigrating through countless rebirths,

sandhāvissaṁ anibbisaṁ; I’ve journeyed without reward.

Tassa me dukkhajātassa, I’ve suffered, but now

dukkhakkhandho aparaddho”ti. the mass of suffering has fallen away.

… Meṇḍasiro thero ….

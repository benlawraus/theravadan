Theragāthā 1.31 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.31

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Catutthavagga Chapter Four

Gahvaratīriyattheragāthā Gahvaratīriya

“Phuṭṭho ḍaṁsehi makasehi, Pestered by flies and mosquitoes

araññasmiṁ brahāvane; in the wilds, the formidable forest,

Nāgo saṅgāmasīseva, one should mindfully endure,

sato tatrādhivāsaye”ti. like an elephant at the head of the battle.

… Gahvaratīriyo thero ….

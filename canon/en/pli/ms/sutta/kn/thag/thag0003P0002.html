Theragāthā 3.2 Verses of the Senior Monks 3.2

Tikanipāta The Book of the Threes

Paṭhamavagga Chapter One

Paccayattheragāthā Paccaya

“Pañcāhāhaṁ pabbajito, I went forth five days ago,

sekho appattamānaso; a trainee, my heart’s desire unfulfilled.

Vihāraṁ me paviṭṭhassa, I entered my dwelling

cetaso paṇidhī ahu. and resolved in my heart:

Nāsissaṁ na pivissāmi, I won’t eat; I won’t drink;

vihārato na nikkhame; I won’t leave my dwelling;

Napi passaṁ nipātessaṁ, nor will I lie down on my side—

taṇhāsalle anūhate. not until the dart of craving is plucked.

Tassa mevaṁ viharato, See my energy and vigor

passa vīriyaparakkamaṁ; as I meditate like this!

Tisso vijjā anuppattā, I’ve attained the three knowledges

kataṁ buddhassa sāsanan”ti. and fulfilled the Buddha’s instructions.

… Paccayo thero ….

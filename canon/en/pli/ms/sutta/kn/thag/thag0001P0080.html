Theragāthā 1.80 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.80

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Aṭṭhamavagga Chapter Eight

Uggattheragāthā Ugga

“Yaṁ mayā pakataṁ kammaṁ, Any deeds I have done,

appaṁ vā yadi vā bahuṁ; a little or a lot,

Sabbametaṁ parikkhīṇaṁ, are all completely exhausted:

natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti. now there’ll be no more future lives.

… Uggo thero …

vaggo aṭṭhamo.

Tassuddānaṁ

Vacchapālo ca yo thero,

ātumo māṇavo isi;

Suyāmano susārado,

thero yo ca piyañjaho;

Ārohaputto meṇḍasiro,

rakkhito uggasavhayoti.

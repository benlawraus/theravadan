Theragāthā 1.113 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.113

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Dvādasamavagga Chapter Twelve

Vanavacchattheragāthā Vanavaccha (2nd)

“Acchodikā puthusilā, The water’s clear and the rocks are broad,

gonaṅgulamigāyutā; monkeys and deer are all around;

Ambusevālasañchannā, festooned with dewy moss,

te selā ramayanti man”ti. these rocky crags delight me!

… Vanavaccho thero ….

Theragāthā 1.84 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.84

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Navamavagga Chapter Nine

Nītattheragāthā Nīta

“Sabbarattiṁ supitvāna, Sleeping all night,

divā saṅgaṇike rato; happily socializing by day,

Kudāssu nāma dummedho, when will the simpleton

dukkhassantaṁ karissatī”ti. make an end of suffering?

… Nīto thero ….

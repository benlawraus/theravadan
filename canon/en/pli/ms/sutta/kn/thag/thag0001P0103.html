Theragāthā 1.103 Verses of the Senior Monks 1.103

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Ekādasamavagga Chapter Eleven

Bandhurattheragāthā Bandhura

“Nāhaṁ etena atthiko, I have no need of this—

Sukhito dhammarasena tappito; I’m happy and satisfied with the sweet teaching.

Pitvā rasaggamuttamaṁ, I’ve drunk the best, the supreme nectar:

Na ca kāhāmi visena santhavan”ti. I won’t go near poison.

… Bandhuro thero ….

Theragāthā 2.31 Verses of the Senior Monks 2.31

Dukanipāta The Book of the Twos

Catutthavagga Chapter Four

Migasirattheragāthā Migasira

“Yato ahaṁ pabbajito, When I had gone forth

sammāsambuddhasāsane; in the teaching of the Buddha,

Vimuccamāno uggacchiṁ, while letting go, I rose up;

kāmadhātuṁ upaccagaṁ. escaping the sensual realm.

Brahmuno pekkhamānassa, Then, as the supreme one looked on,

tato cittaṁ vimucci me; my mind was freed.

Akuppā me vimuttīti, My freedom is unshakable

sabbasaṁyojanakkhayā”ti. with the ending of all fetters.

… Migasiro thero ….

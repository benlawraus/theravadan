Upasamātherīgāthā
Verses of the Elder Upasamā
Upasamā

“Upasame tare oghaṁ,
“Upasamā, pass over the flood,
Upasamā, cross the flood,

maccudheyyaṁ suduttaraṁ;
the sphere of death so difficult to cross.
Death’s dominion so hard to pass.

Dhārehi antimaṁ dehaṁ,
Carry your last body,
When you have vanquished Māra and his mount,

jetvā māraṁ savāhinin”ti.
having conquered Māra and his army.”
bear your final body.

… Upasamā therī ….
The Elder Upasamā

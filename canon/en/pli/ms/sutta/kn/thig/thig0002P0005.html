Therīgāthā 2.5 Verses of the Elder Bhikkhunīs Verses of the Senior Nuns 2.5

Dukanipāta Chapter of the Twos The Book of the Twos

Cittātherīgāthā Verses of the Elder Cittā Cittā

“Kiñcāpi khomhi kisikā, “I was skin and bones, Though I’m skinny,

gilānā bāḷhadubbalā; sick, and very weak; sick, and very feeble,

Daṇḍamolubbha gacchāmi, but, leaning on a stick, I climb the mountain,

pabbataṁ abhirūhiya. I climbed the mountain. leaning on a staff.

Saṅghāṭiṁ nikkhipitvāna, I laid down the outer robe Having laid down my outer robe,

pattakañca nikujjiya; and overturned the bowl; and overturned my bowl,

Sele khambhesimattānaṁ, settled on a stone, propping myself against a rock,

tamokhandhaṁ padāliyā”ti. I destroyed this mass of darkness.” I shattered the mass of darkness.

… Cittā therī …. The Elder Cittā

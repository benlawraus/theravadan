Therīgāthā 1.13 Verses of the Elder Bhikkhunīs Verses of the Senior Nuns 1.13

Ekakanipāta Chapter of the Ones The Book of the Ones

Visākhātherīgāthā The Elder Visākhā Visākhā

“Karotha buddhasāsanaṁ, “Do what the Buddha taught Do the Buddha’s bidding,

yaṁ katvā nānutappati; and you will have no regrets. you won’t regret it.

Khippaṁ pādāni dhovitvā, Quickly wash your feet Having quickly washed your feet,

ekamante nisīdathā”ti. and sit down to one side.” sit in a discreet place to meditate.

… Visākhā therī …. The Elder Visākhā

Therīgāthā 1.5 Verses of the Elder Bhikkhunīs Verses of the Senior Nuns 1.5

Ekakanipāta Chapter of the Ones The Book of the Ones

Aññatarātissātherīgāthā Verses of Another Elder Named Tissā Another Tissā

“Tisse yuñjassu dhammehi, “Tissā, bind yourself to the Dhamma; Tissā, apply yourself to good qualities—

khaṇo taṁ mā upaccagā; waste no time. don’t let the moment pass you by.

Khaṇātītā hi socanti, Those who waste time sorrow For if you miss your moment,

nirayamhi samappitā”ti. when they arrive in hell.” you’ll grieve when sent to hell.

Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 51

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Āsīsavagga Hope

1. Mahāsīlavajātaka The Great Virtuous One: a past life story

“Āsīsetheva puriso, A man should have hope,

na nibbindeyya paṇḍito; the astute should not be disillusioned.

Passāmi vohaṁ attānaṁ, Now I truly see myself—

yathā icchiṁ tathā ahū”ti. as I wished, so I became.

Mahāsīlavajātakaṁ paṭhamaṁ.

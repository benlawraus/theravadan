Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Parosatavagga

4. Mittavindakajātaka

“Catubbhi aṭṭhajjhagamā,

aṭṭhāhipi ca soḷasa;

Soḷasāhi ca bāttiṁsa,

atricchaṁ cakkamāsado;

Icchāhatassa posassa,

cakkaṁ bhamati matthake”ti.

Mittavindakajātakaṁ catutthaṁ.

Jātaka

Dukanipāta

Kalyāṇavagga

7. Tindukajātaka

“Dhanuhatthakalāpehi,

nettiṁ savaradhāribhi;

Samantā parikiṇṇamha,

kathaṁ mokkho bhavissati”.

“Appeva bahukiccānaṁ,

attho jāyetha koci naṁ;

Atthi rukkhassa acchinnaṁ,

khajjathaññeva tindukan”ti.

Tindukajātakaṁ sattamaṁ.

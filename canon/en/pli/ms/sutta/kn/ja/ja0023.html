Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 23

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Kuruṅgavagga The Four-horned Antelope

3. Bhojājānīyajātaka The King’s Thoroughbred

“Api passena semāno, Though lying on my side

sallebhi sallalīkato; pierced by many arrows,

Seyyova vaḷavā bhojjo, the king’s stallion beats a gelding—

yuñja maññeva sārathī”ti. harness me now, charioteer.

Bhojājānīyajātakaṁ tatiyaṁ.

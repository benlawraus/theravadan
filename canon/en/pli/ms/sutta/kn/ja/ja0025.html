Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 25

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Kuruṅgavagga The Four-horned Antelope

5. Titthajātaka Fords: a past life story

“Aññamaññehi titthehi, At one ford or another

assaṁ pāyehi sārathi; lead the horse to drink, rider.

Accāsanassa puriso, For man who overeats,

pāyāsassapi tappatī”ti. even milk-rice is a torment.

Titthajātakaṁ pañcamaṁ.

Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 7

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Apaṇṇakavagga Unfailing

7. Kaṭṭhahārijātaka The Wood Gatherer: a past life story

“Putto tyāhaṁ mahārāja, I am your son, great king!

tvaṁ maṁ posa janādhipa; Provide for me, ruler of the people.

Aññepi devo poseti, The king provides for others,

kiñca devo sakaṁ pajan”ti. what then of his own offspring?

Kaṭṭhahārijātakaṁ sattamaṁ.

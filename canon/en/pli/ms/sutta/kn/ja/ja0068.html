8. Sāketajātaka
At Sāketa: a past life story

Yasmiṁ mano nivisati,
In whom the mind settles,

cittañcāpi pasīdati;
and the heart too is confident—

Adiṭṭhapubbake pose,
gladly one would trust in

kāmaṁ tasmimpi vissase”ti.
that man not seen before.

Sāketajātakaṁ aṭṭhamaṁ.

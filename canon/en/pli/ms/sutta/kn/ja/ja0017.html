Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 17

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Sīlavagga Ethics

7. Mālutajātaka Gales: a past life story

“Kāḷe vā yadi vā juṇhe, Whether the moon is waning or waxing,

yadā vāyati māluto; it is cool when the gale blows.

Vātajāni hi sītāni, Since the wind brings the cool,

ubhotthamaparājitā”ti. both are undefeated in this instance.

Mālutajātakaṁ sattamaṁ.

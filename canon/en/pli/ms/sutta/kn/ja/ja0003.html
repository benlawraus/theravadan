Apaṇṇakavagga
Unfailing

3. Serivavāṇijajātaka
The Wanton Merchant: a past life story

“Idha ce naṁ virādhesi,
If you fail to achieve

saddhammassa niyāmataṁ;
certainty regarding the true teaching

Ciraṁ tvaṁ anutappesi,
you’ll regret it for a long time,

serivāyaṁva vāṇijo”ti.
like this wanton merchant.

Serivavāṇijajātakaṁ tatiyaṁ.

Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 69

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Itthivagga Women

9. Visavantajātaka Vomited Poison: a past life story

“Dhiratthu taṁ visaṁ vantaṁ, Curse that vomited poison,

yamahaṁ jīvitakāraṇā; which I vomited for the sake of life

Vantaṁ paccāvamissāmi, and now shall consume again.

mataṁ me jīvitā varan”ti. Death is better for me than life!

Visavantajātakaṁ navamaṁ.

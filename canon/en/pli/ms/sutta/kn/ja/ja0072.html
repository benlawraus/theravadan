Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 72

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Varuṇavagga The Warding Tree

2. Sīlavahatthijātaka The Virtuous Elephant: a past life story

“Akataññussa posassa, An ungrateful person,

niccaṁ vivaradassino; always looking for opportunity,

Sabbañce pathaviṁ dajjā, even if given the whole earth,

neva naṁ abhirādhaye”ti. would still not be satisfied.

Sīlavahatthijātakaṁ dutiyaṁ.

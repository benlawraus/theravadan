Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 74

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Varuṇavagga The Warding Tree

4. Rukkhadhammajātaka The Law of Trees: a past life story

“Sādhū sambahulā ñātī, It is well for the many relatives,

api rukkhā araññajā; the trees born in the forest.

Vāto vahati ekaṭṭhaṁ, The wind sweeps off one standing alone,

brahantampi vanappatin”ti. even the enormous lord of the forest.

Rukkhadhammajātakaṁ catutthaṁ.

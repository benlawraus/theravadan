9. Nandajātaka
Nanda: a past life story

“Maññe sovaṇṇayo rāsi,
I suspect the heap of gold

soṇṇamālā ca nandako;
and golden chains

Yattha dāso āmajāto,
is where the house-born slave Nandaka

ṭhito thullāni gajjatī”ti.
stands yelling curses.

Nandajātakaṁ navamaṁ.

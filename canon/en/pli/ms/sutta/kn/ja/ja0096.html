Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Littavagga

6. Telapattajātaka

“Samatittikaṁ anavasekaṁ,

Telapattaṁ yathā parihareyya;

Evaṁ sacittamanurakkhe,

Patthayāno disaṁ agatapubban”ti.

Telapattajātakaṁ chaṭṭhaṁ.

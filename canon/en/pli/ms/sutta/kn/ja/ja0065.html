Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 65

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Itthivagga Women

5. Anabhiratijātaka Dissatisfaction: a past life story

“Yathā nadī ca pantho ca, Just like rivers and roads,

pānāgāraṁ sabhā papā; taverns, hotels, and pubs,

Evaṁ lokitthiyo nāma, are those called women in the world.

nāsaṁ kujjhanti paṇḍitā”ti. Sages do not get angry with them.

Anabhiratijātakaṁ pañcamaṁ.

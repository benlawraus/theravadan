Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 66

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Itthivagga Women

6. Mudulakkhaṇajātaka Queen Sweetheart: a past life story

“Ekā icchā pure āsi, One wish I had in the past,

aladdhā mudulakkhaṇaṁ; before I won Queen Sweetheart.

Yato laddhā aḷārakkhī, But when I won the moon-eyed lady,

icchā icchaṁ vijāyathā”ti. wish upon wish was born.

Mudulakkhaṇajātakaṁ chaṭṭhaṁ.

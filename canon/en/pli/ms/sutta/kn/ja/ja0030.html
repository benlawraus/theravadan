Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 30

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Kuruṅgavagga The Four-horned Antelope

10. Munikajātaka The Pig Named Munika: a past life story

“Mā munikassa pihayi, Envy not what is Munika’s,

āturannāni bhuñjati; for he eats the food of the terminally ill.

Appossukko bhusaṁ khāda, Don’t worry, just eat your chaff,

etaṁ dīghāyulakkhaṇan”ti. this is a mark of long life.

Munikajātakaṁ dasamaṁ.

Kuruṅgavaggo tatiyo.

Tassuddānaṁ

Kuruṅgassa kukkuragojavaro,

Puna vāḷavassasirivhayano;

Mahiḷāmukhanāmanuññavaro,

Vahate dhura munikena dasāti.

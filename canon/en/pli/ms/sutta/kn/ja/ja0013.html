Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 13

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Sīlavagga Ethics

3. Kaṇḍijātaka The Arrow: a past life story

“Dhiratthu kaṇḍinaṁ sallaṁ, Curse the dart, the arrow

purisaṁ gāḷhavedhinaṁ; that deeply strikes a man.

Dhiratthu taṁ janapadaṁ, Curse the nation

yatthitthī pariṇāyikā; where women rule.

Te cāpi dhikkitā sattā, Those creatures are cursed

ye itthīnaṁ vasaṅgatā”ti. who fall to women’s sway.

Kaṇḍijātakaṁ tatiyaṁ.

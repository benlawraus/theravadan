Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 26

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Kuruṅgavagga The Four-horned Antelope

6. Mahiḷāmukhajātaka The Elephant Named Ladyface: a past life story

“Purāṇacorāna vaco nisamma, Before, hearing the speech of the bandits,

Mahiḷāmukho pothayamanvacārī; Ladyface went on a rampage.

Susaññatānañhi vaco nisamma, But hearing the speech of the well-restrained,

Gajuttamo sabbaguṇesu aṭṭhā”ti. the supreme elephant is established in all good qualities.

Mahiḷāmukhajātakaṁ chaṭṭhaṁ.

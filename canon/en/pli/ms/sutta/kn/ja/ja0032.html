Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 7

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Kulāvakavagga Nests

2. Naccajātaka Dancing: a past life story

“Rudaṁ manuññaṁ rucirā ca piṭṭhi, Your cry is sweet, your back brilliant,

Veḷuriyavaṇṇūpanibhā ca gīvā; your neck the color of sapphire,

Byāmamattāni ca pekhuṇāni, and your feathers a full fathom long;

Naccena te dhītaraṁ no dadāmī”ti. still, because of your dancing, I will not give you my daughter.

Naccajātakaṁ dutiyaṁ.

Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 31

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Kulāvakavagga Nests

1. Kulāvakajātaka Nests: a past life story

“Kulāvakā mātali simbalismiṁ, Mātali, don’t ram the bird nests

Īsāmukhena parivajjayassu; in the red silk-cotton woods with your chariot pole.

Kāmaṁ cajāma asuresu pāṇaṁ, I’d rather give up our lives to the titans

Māme dijā vikkulāvā ahesun”ti. than deprive these birds of their nests.

Kulāvakajātakaṁ paṭhamaṁ.

4. Kiṁphalajātaka
The What-fruit: a past life story

“Nāyaṁ rukkho durāruho,
This tree is not hard to climb,

napi gāmato ārakā;
nor is it far from the village.

Ākāraṇena jānāmi,
For this reason I know

nāyaṁ sāduphalo dumo”ti.
this tree is not sweet-fruited.

Kiṁphalajātakaṁ catutthaṁ.

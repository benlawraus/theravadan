Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 53

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Āsīsavagga Hope

3. Puṇṇapātijātaka The Full Cups: a past life story

“Tatheva puṇṇā pātiyo, The cups stay just as full,

aññāyaṁ vattate kathā; while the ignorant talk goes on.

Ākāraṇena jānāmi, For this reason I know

na cāyaṁ bhaddikā surā”ti. this is not an excellent beer.

Puṇṇapātijātakaṁ tatiyaṁ.

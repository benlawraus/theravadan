Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 75

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Varuṇavagga The Warding Tree

5. Macchajātaka The Fish: a past life story

“Abhitthanaya pajjunna, Thunder forth, Pajjuna!

nidhiṁ kākassa nāsaya; Wipe out the trove of the crow!

Kākaṁ sokāya randhehi, Subject the crow to sorrow,

mañca sokā pamocayā”ti. and free me from sorrow!

Macchajātakaṁ pañcamaṁ.

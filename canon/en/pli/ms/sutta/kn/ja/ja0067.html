Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 67

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Itthivagga Women

7. Ucchaṅgajātaka In My Lap: a past life story

“Ucchaṅge deva me putto, A son in my lap, your highness,

Pathe dhāvantiyā pati; or a husband while walking the street—

Tañca desaṁ na passāmi, but I don’t see the place

Yato sodariyamānayeti. from where I can fetch a brother.

Ucchaṅgajātakaṁ sattamaṁ.

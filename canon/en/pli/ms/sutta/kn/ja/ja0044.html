Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 44

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Atthakāmavagga Desiring the Good

4. Makasajātaka The Mosquito: a past life story

“Seyyo amitto matiyā upeto, Better is a considerate foe

Na tveva mitto mativippahīno; than an inconsiderate friend.

Makasaṁ vadhissanti hi eḷamūgo, Thinking, “I shall kill the mosquito”,

Putto pitu abbhidā uttamaṅgan”ti. the idiot son split open his father’s head.

Makasajātakaṁ catutthaṁ.

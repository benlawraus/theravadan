Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 35

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Kulāvakavagga Nests

5. Vaṭṭakajātaka The Quail: a past life story

“Santi pakkhā apatanā, I have wings that do not fly!

santi pādā avañcanā; I have feet that do not walk!

Mātāpitā ca nikkhantā, Mother and father have fled!

jātaveda paṭikkamā”ti. Jātaveda the fire: go back!

Vaṭṭakajātakaṁ pañcamaṁ.

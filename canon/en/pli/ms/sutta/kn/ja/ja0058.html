Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 58

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Āsīsavagga Hope

8. Tayodhammajātaka Three Qualities: a past life story

“Yassa ete tayo dhammā, Whoever has these three qualities

vānarinda yathā tava; like you, lord of langurs—

Dakkhiyaṁ sūriyaṁ paññā, adroitness, heroism, wisdom—

diṭṭhaṁ so ativattatī”ti. escapes the visible foe.

Tayodhammajātakaṁ aṭṭhamaṁ.

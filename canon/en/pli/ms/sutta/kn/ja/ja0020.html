Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 20

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Sīlavagga Ethics

10. Naḷapānajātaka The Reed Drink: a past life story

“Disvā padamanuttiṇṇaṁ, Seeing footprints go down into the water,

Disvānotaritaṁ padaṁ; and none that return,

Naḷena vāriṁ pissāma, we shall drink water through a reed:

Neva maṁ tvaṁ vadhissasī”ti. no way shall you kill me.

Naḷapānajātakaṁ dasamaṁ.

Sīlavaggo dutiyo.

Tassuddānaṁ

Atha lakkhaṇa sākha dhiratthu puna,

Na kiratthi rasehi kharādiyā;

Atibhoti vara māluta pāṇa,

Muccena naḷaavhayanena bhavanti dasāti.

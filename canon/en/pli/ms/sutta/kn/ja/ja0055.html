Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 55

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Āsīsavagga Hope

5. Pañcāvudhajātaka Prince Five-Weapons: a past life story

“Yo alīnena cittena, He whose heart is not stuck,

alīnamanaso naro; a man of intrepid mind,

Bhāveti kusalaṁ dhammaṁ, develops skilful qualities

yogakkhemassa pattiyā; for the sake of sanctuary from the yoke.

Pāpuṇe anupubbena, Gradually he would attain

sabbasaṁyojanakkhayan”ti. the ending of all fetters.

Pañcāvudhajātakaṁ pañcamaṁ.

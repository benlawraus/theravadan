Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 45

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Atthakāmavagga Desiring the Good

5. Rohiṇijātaka About Rohinī: a past life story

“Seyyo amitto medhāvī, Better is an intelligent foe

Yañce bālānukampako; than a foolish sympathizer.

Passa rohiṇikaṁ jammiṁ, See that wretched Rohinī,

Mātaraṁ hantvāna socatī”ti. grieving after killing her mother.

Rohiṇijātakaṁ pañcamaṁ.

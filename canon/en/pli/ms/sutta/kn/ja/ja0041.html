Ekakanipāta
The Book of the Ones

Atthakāmavagga
Desiring the Good

1. Losakajātaka
About Losaka: a past life story

“Yo atthakāmassa hitānukampino,
One who does not follow advice when instructed

Ovajjamāno na karoti sāsanaṁ;
by an ally who desires their good,

Ajiyā pādamolamba,
grieves like Mittaka

Mittako viya socatī”ti.
hanging off a goat’s foot.

Losakajātakaṁ paṭhamaṁ.

Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 24

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Kuruṅgavagga The Four-horned Antelope

4. Ājaññajātaka The Thoroughbred: a past life story

“Yadā yadā yattha yadā, Whenever whenever, wherever whenever,

yattha yattha yadā yadā; wherever wherever, whenever whenever:

Ājañño kurute vegaṁ, the thoroughbred drives onward,

hāyanti tattha vāḷavā”ti. while the geldings fade right there.

Ājaññajātakaṁ catutthaṁ.

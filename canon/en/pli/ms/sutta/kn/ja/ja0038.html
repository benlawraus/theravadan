Jātaka Stories of Past Lives 38

Ekakanipāta The Book of the Ones

Kulāvakavagga Nests

8. Bakajātaka The Crane: a past life story

“Nāccantaṁ nikatippañño, The clever cheat cannot achieve

nikatyā sukhamedhati; ultimate happiness by cheating.

Ārādheti nikatippañño, The clever cheat succeeds only

bako kakkaṭakāmivā”ti. in getting what the crane did from the crab.

Bakajātakaṁ aṭṭhamaṁ.

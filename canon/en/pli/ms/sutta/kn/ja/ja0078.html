8. Illisajātaka
Herring, the Miser: a past life story

“Ubho khañjā ubho kuṇī,
Both are lame, both crippled,

ubho visamacakkhukā;
both cross-eyed,

Ubhinnaṁ piḷakā jātā,
and both covered in warts:

nāhaṁ passāmi illisan”ti.
I see not which is Herring.

Illisajātakaṁ aṭṭhamaṁ.

Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Apāyimhavagga

3. Kāḷakaṇṇijātaka

“Mitto have sattapadena hoti,

Sahāyo pana dvādasakena hoti;

Māsaḍḍhamāsena ca ñāti hoti,

Tatuttariṁ attasamopi hoti;

Sohaṁ kathaṁ attasukhassa hetu,

Cirasanthutaṁ kāḷakaṇṇiṁ jaheyyan”ti.

Kāḷakaṇṇijātakaṁ tatiyaṁ

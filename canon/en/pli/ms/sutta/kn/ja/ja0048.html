Atthakāmavagga
Desiring the Good

8. Vedabbajātaka
About Vedabbha: a past life story

“Anupāyena yo atthaṁ,
Who wishes to profit by inappropriate means

Icchati so vihaññati;
is tormented.

Cetā haniṁsu vedabbaṁ,
The Cetans who killed Vedabbha

Sabbe te byasanamajjhagūti.
all fell into ruin.

Vedabbajātakaṁ aṭṭhamaṁ.

14. Kukkuḷavagga
14. Burning Chaff

Tatiyadukkhasutta
Suffering (3rd)

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.
At Sāvatthī.

“Yaṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo …pe…
“Mendicants, you should give up desire and greed for what is suffering. …”

yaṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo”ti.

Sattamaṁ.

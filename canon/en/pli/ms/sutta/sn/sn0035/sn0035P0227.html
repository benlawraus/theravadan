Saṁyutta Nikāya 35.227 Linked Discourses 35.227

17. Saṭṭhipeyyālavagga 17. Sixty Abbreviated Texts

Bāhirāyatanaanattasutta The Exterior as Not-Self

“Rūpā, bhikkhave, anattā. Saddā … gandhā … rasā … phoṭṭhabbā … dhammā anattā. “Mendicants, sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, and ideas are not-self.

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe… Seeing this …

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti. They understand: ‘… there is nothing further for this place.’”

Saṭṭhipeyyālo samatto.

Tassuddānaṁ

Chandenaṭṭhārasa honti,

atītena ca dve nava;

Yadaniccāṭṭhārasa vuttā,

tayo ajjhattabāhirā;

Peyyālo saṭṭhiko vutto,

buddhenādiccabandhunāti.

Suttantāni saṭṭhi.

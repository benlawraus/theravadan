13. Oghavagga
13. Floods

Oghādisutta
Floods

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, oghā.
“Mendicants, there are these four floods.

Katame cattāro?
What four?

Kāmogho, bhavogho, diṭṭhogho, avijjogho”ti.
The floods of sensuality, desire to be reborn, views, and ignorance.”

vitthāretabbaṁ.
(Tell in full as in SN 45.171–179.)

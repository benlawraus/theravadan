14. Punagaṅgāpeyyālavagga 14. Another Chapter of Abbreviated Texts on the Ganges

Punagaṅgānadīādisutta More on the Ganges River, Etc.

…pe…

vaggo cuddasamo.

Uddānaṁ

Cha pācīnato ninnā,

cha ninnā ca samuddato;

Dvete cha dvādasa honti,

vaggo tena pavuccatīti.

(Bojjhaṅgasaṁyuttassa gaṅgāpeyyālaṁ rāgavasena vitthāretabbaṁ.) (Tell in full as in SN 45.103–114, removal of greed version.)

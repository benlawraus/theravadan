Saṁyutta Nikāya 46.10
Linked Discourses 46.10

1. Pabbatavagga
1. Mountains

Dutiyauppannasutta
Arisen (2nd)

“Sattime, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā anuppannā uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā.
“Mendicants, these seven awakening factors don’t arise to be developed and cultivated apart from the Holy One’s training.

Katame satta?
What seven?

Satisambojjhaṅgo …pe… upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—
The awakening factors of mindfulness, investigation of principles, energy, rapture, tranquility, immersion, and equanimity.

ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā anuppannā uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā”ti.
These seven awakening factors don’t arise to be developed and cultivated apart from the Holy One’s training.”

Dasamaṁ.

Pabbatavaggo paṭhamo.

Tassuddānaṁ

Himavantaṁ kāyaṁ sīlaṁ,

vatthaṁ bhikkhu ca kuṇḍali;

Kūṭañca upavānañca,

uppannā apare duveti.

3. Udāyivagga
3. With Udāyī

Bojjhaṅgadesanāsutta
A Teaching on the Awakening Factors

“Satta vo, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅge desessāmi;
“Mendicants, I will teach you the seven awakening factors.

taṁ suṇātha.
Listen …

Katame ca, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā?
And what are the seven awakening factors?

Satisambojjhaṅgo …pe… upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—
The awakening factors of mindfulness, investigation of principles, energy, rapture, tranquility, immersion, and equanimity.

ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā”ti.
These are the seven awakening factors.”

Dutiyaṁ.

Saṁyutta Nikāya 42.4 Linked Discourses 42.4

1. Gāmaṇivagga 1. Chiefs

Hatthārohasutta An Elephant Warrior

Atha kho hatthāroho gāmaṇi yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā …pe… Then an elephant warrior chief went up to the Buddha …

ajjatagge pāṇupetaṁ saraṇaṁ gatanti. “From this day forth, may the Buddha remember me as a lay follower who has gone for refuge for life.”

Catutthaṁ.

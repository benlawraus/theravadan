Saṁyutta Nikāya 55.61 Linked Discourses 55.61

6. Sappaññavagga 6. A Wise Person

Paññāvepullasutta The Increase of Wisdom

“…. Paññāvepullāya … saṁvattantī”ti. “Mendicants, when four things are developed and cultivated they lead to the increase of wisdom. …”

Ekādasamaṁ.

Sappaññavaggo chaṭṭho.

Tassuddānaṁ

Sagāthakaṁ vassaṁvutthaṁ,

dhammadinnañca gilānaṁ;

Caturo phalā paṭilābho,

vuddhi vepullatāya cāti.

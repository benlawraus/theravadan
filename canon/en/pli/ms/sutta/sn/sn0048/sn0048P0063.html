Saṁyutta Nikāya 48.63 Linked Discourses 48.63

7. Bodhipakkhiyavagga 7. Leading to Awakening

Pariññāsutta Complete Understanding

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni addhānapariññāya saṁvattanti. “Mendicants, when these five faculties are developed and cultivated they lead to the complete understanding of the course of time.

Katamāni pañca? What five?

Saddhindriyaṁ …pe… paññindriyaṁ—The faculties of faith, energy, mindfulness, immersion, and wisdom.

imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni addhānapariññāya saṁvattantī”ti. When these five faculties are developed and cultivated they lead to the complete understanding of the course of time.”

Tatiyaṁ.

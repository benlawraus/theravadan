11. Esanāvagga 11. The Chapter on Searches

Esanāvagga The Chapter on Searches

(Esanāvaggo vitthāretabbo.) (Tell in full as in the chapter on searches at SN 45.161–170.)

Tassuddānaṁ

Esanā vidhā āsavo, Searches, discriminations, defilements,

Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso; states of existence, three kinds of suffering,

Khilaṁ malañca nīgho ca, barrenness, stains, and troubles,

Vedanā taṇhā tasinā cāti. feelings, craving, and thirst.

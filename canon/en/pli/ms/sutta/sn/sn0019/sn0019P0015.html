Saṁyutta Nikāya 19.15 Linked Discourses 19.15

2. Dutiyavagga Chapter Two

Okilinīsutta A Sweltering Woman

“Idhāhaṁ, āvuso, gijjhakūṭā pabbatā orohanto addasaṁ itthiṁ uppakkaṁ okiliniṁ okiriniṁ vehāsaṁ gacchantiṁ. “Just now, reverend, as I was descending from Vulture’s Peak Mountain I saw a scorched woman, sooty and sweaty, flying through the air,

Sā sudaṁ aṭṭassaraṁ karoti …pe… as she screamed in pain. …” …

esā, bhikkhave, itthī kaliṅgassa rañño aggamahesī ahosi. “That woman used to be the king of Kaliṅga’s chief queen.

Sā issāpakatā sapattiṁ aṅgārakaṭāhena okiri …pe…. She was of jealous nature, and poured a brazier of hot coals over her co-wife. …” …

Pañcamaṁ.

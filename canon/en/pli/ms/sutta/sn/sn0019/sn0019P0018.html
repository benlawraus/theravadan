2. Dutiyavagga
Chapter Two

Pāpabhikkhunīsutta
A Bad Nun

“Addasaṁ bhikkhuniṁ vehāsaṁ gacchantiṁ.
“I saw a nun flying through the air.

Tassā saṅghāṭipi ādittā …pe…
Her outer robe was burning …” …

pāpabhikkhunī ahosi …pe….
“She used to be a bad nun …”

Aṭṭhamaṁ.

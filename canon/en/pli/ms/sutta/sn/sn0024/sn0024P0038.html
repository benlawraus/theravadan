Saṁyutta Nikāya 24.38
Linked Discourses 24.38

2. Dutiyagamanavagga
2. The Second Round

Arūpīattāsutta
The Self Is Formless

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.
At Sāvatthī.

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:
“Mendicants, when what exists, because of grasping what and insisting on what, does the view arise:

‘arūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?
‘The self is formless and healthy after death’?” …

(Peyyālo.)

Vīsatimaṁ.

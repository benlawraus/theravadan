Saṁyutta Nikāya 43.13
Linked Discourses 43.13

2. Dutiyavagga
Chapter Two

Anatasutta
Uninclined

“Anatañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, anatagāmiñca maggaṁ.
“Mendicants, I will teach you the uninclined …”

Taṁ suṇātha.

Katamañca, bhikkhave, anataṁ …pe…”.

(Yathā asaṅkhataṁ tathā vitthāretabbaṁ.)
(Tell in full as in the unconditioned in the previous chapter.)

Dutiyaṁ.

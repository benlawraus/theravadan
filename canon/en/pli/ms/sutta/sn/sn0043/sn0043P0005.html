Saṁyutta Nikāya 43.5 Linked Discourses 43.5

1. Paṭhamavagga Chapter One

Satipaṭṭhānasutta Mindfulness Meditation

“Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? “And what is the path that leads to the unconditioned?

Cattāro satipaṭṭhānā. Ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo …pe…. The four kinds of mindfulness meditation. …”

Pañcamaṁ.

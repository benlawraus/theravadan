Saṁyutta Nikāya 45.13 Linked Discourses 45.13

2. Vihāravagga 2. Meditation

Sekkhasutta A Trainee

Sāvatthinidānaṁ. At Sāvatthī.

Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami …pe… ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca: Then a mendicant went up to the Buddha … and asked him,

“‘sekkho, sekkho’ti, bhante, vuccati. “Sir, they speak of this person called ‘a trainee’.

Kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, sekkho hotī”ti? How is a trainee defined?”

“Idha, bhikkhu, sekkhāya sammādiṭṭhiyā samannāgato hoti …pe… sekkhena sammāsamādhinā samannāgato hoti. “Mendicant, it’s someone who has a trainee’s right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right immersion.

Ettāvatā kho, bhikkhu, sekkho hotī”ti. That’s how a trainee is defined.”

Tatiyaṁ.

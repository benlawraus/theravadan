Saṁyutta Nikāya 15.15
Linked Discourses 15.15

2. Dutiyavagga
Chapter Two

Pitusutta
Father

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.
At Sāvatthī.

“Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro …pe…
“Mendicants, transmigration has no known beginning. …

na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo napitābhūtapubbo …pe…
It’s not easy to find a sentient being who in all this long time has not previously been your father. …

alaṁ vimuccitun”ti.
This is quite enough for you to become disillusioned, dispassionate, and freed regarding all conditions.”

Pañcamaṁ.

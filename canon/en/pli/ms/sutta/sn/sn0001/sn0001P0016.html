Saṁyutta Nikāya 1.16 Linked Discourses 1.16

2. Nandanavagga 2. The Garden of Delight

Niddātandīsutta Sleepiness and Sloth Drowsiness and Lethargy

“Niddā tandī vijambhitā, “Sleepiness, sloth, and yawning, “Drowsiness, lethargy, lazy stretching,

Aratī bhattasammado; discontent, and grogginess after eating: Discontent, torpor after meals:

Etena nappakāsati, because of this the noble path Because of this, it does not appear:

Ariyamaggo idha pāṇinan”ti. doesn’t shine for creatures here.” The noble path, here among beings. ”

“Niddaṁ tandiṁ vijambhitaṁ, “Sleepiness, sloth, and yawning, “Drowsiness, lethargy, lazy stretching,

Aratiṁ bhattasammadaṁ; discontent, and grogginess after eating: Discontent, torpor after meals:

Vīriyena naṁ paṇāmetvā, when this is energetically fended off, When one dispels this with energy,

Ariyamaggo visujjhatī”ti. the noble path is purified.” The noble path is cleared.

Saṁyutta Nikāya 1.44 Linked Discourses 1.44

5. Ādittavagga 5. On Fire

Ekamūlasutta One Root A devatā:“One Root

“Ekamūlaṁ dvirāvaṭṭaṁ, “One the root, two the loops, With its one root, two whirlpools,

timalaṁ pañcapattharaṁ; three the stains, five the spreads, Three stains, five extensions,

Samuddaṁ dvādasāvaṭṭaṁ, and the ocean, the twelfth whirlpool: An ocean with twelve eddies.

pātālaṁ atarī isī”ti. such is the abyss crossed over by the seer.” The seer has crossed over the abyss.”

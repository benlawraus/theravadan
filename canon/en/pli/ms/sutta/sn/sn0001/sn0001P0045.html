Saṁyutta Nikāya 1.45 Linked Discourses 1.45

5. Ādittavagga 5. On Fire

Anomasutta Peerless Perfect

“Anomanāmaṁ nipuṇatthadassiṁ, Behold him of peerless name who sees the subtle meaning, A devatā: “Behold him of perfect name, The seer of the subtle goal,

Paññādadaṁ kāmālaye asattaṁ; giver of wisdom, unattached to the realm of sensuality: The giver of wisdom, unattached To the lair of sensual pleasures.

Taṁ passatha sabbaviduṁ sumedhaṁ, the all-knower, so very intelligent, Behold the wise one, all-knowing,

Ariye pathe kamamānaṁ mahesin”ti. the great seer treading the noble road.” The great seer treading the noble path. ”

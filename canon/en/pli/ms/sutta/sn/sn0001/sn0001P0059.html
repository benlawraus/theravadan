Saṁyutta Nikāya 1.59 Linked Discourses 1.59

6. Jarāvagga 6. Old Age

Dutiyasutta A Partner Partner

“Kiṁsu dutiyā purisassa hoti, “What is a person’s partner? “What is a person’s partner?

Kiṁsu cenaṁ pasāsati; What instructs them? What is it that instructs him?

Kissa cābhirato macco, Enjoying what is a mortal Taking delight in what is a mortal

Sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti. released from all suffering?” Released from all suffering?”

“Saddhā dutiyā purisassa hoti, “Faith is a person’s partner. “Faith is a person’s partner,

Paññā cenaṁ pasāsati; Wisdom instructs them. And wisdom is what instructs him.

Nibbānābhirato macco, Delighting in extinguishment a mortal Taking delight in Nibbāna, a mortal

Sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti. is released from all suffering.” Is released from all suffering. ”

Aṅguttara Nikāya 5.25 Numbered Discourses 5.25

3. Pañcaṅgikavagga 3. With Five Factors

Anuggahitasutta Supported

“Pañcahi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi anuggahitā sammādiṭṭhi cetovimuttiphalā ca hoti cetovimuttiphalānisaṁsā ca, paññāvimuttiphalā ca hoti paññāvimuttiphalānisaṁsā ca. “Mendicants, when right view is supported by five factors it has freedom of heart and freedom by wisdom as its fruit and benefit.

Katamehi pañcahi? What five?

Idha, bhikkhave, sammādiṭṭhi sīlānuggahitā ca hoti, sutānuggahitā ca hoti, sākacchānuggahitā ca hoti, samathānuggahitā ca hoti, vipassanānuggahitā ca hoti. It’s when right view is supported by ethics, learning, discussion, serenity, and discernment.

Imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi aṅgehi anuggahitā sammādiṭṭhi cetovimuttiphalā ca hoti cetovimuttiphalānisaṁsā ca, paññāvimuttiphalā ca hoti paññāvimuttiphalānisaṁsā cā”ti. When right view is supported by these five factors it has freedom of heart and freedom by wisdom as its fruit and benefit.”

Pañcamaṁ.

Aṅguttara Nikāya 5.182
Numbered Discourses 5.182

19. Araññavagga
19. Wilderness Dwellers

Cīvarasutta
Robes

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, paṁsukūlikā.
“Mendicants, these five wear rag robes.

Katame pañca?
What five?

Mandattā momūhattā paṁsukūliko hoti …pe… idamatthitaṁyeva nissāya paṁsukūliko hoti.
A person may wear rag robes because of stupidity … corrupt wishes … madness … because it is praised by the Buddhas … or for the sake of having few wishes …

Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca paṁsukūlikā”ti.
These are the five who wear rag robes.”

Dutiyaṁ.

Aṅguttara Nikāya 5.252 Numbered Discourses 5.252

26. Upasampadāvagga 26. Ordination

Nissayasutta Who Should Give Dependence

“Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgatena bhikkhunā nissayo dātabbo. “Mendicants, dependence should be given by a mendicant with five qualities.

Katamehi pañcahi? What five?

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu asekhena sīlakkhandhena samannāgato hoti …pe… asekhena vimuttiñāṇadassanakkhandhena samannāgato hoti. It’s a mendicant who has the entire spectrum of an adept’s ethics, immersion, wisdom, freedom, and the knowledge and vision of freedom.

Imehi …pe… nissayo dātabbo”ti. Dependence should be given by a mendicant with these five qualities.”

Dutiyaṁ.

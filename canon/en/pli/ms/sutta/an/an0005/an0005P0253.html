Aṅguttara Nikāya 5.253
Numbered Discourses 5.253

26. Upasampadāvagga
26. Ordination

Sāmaṇerasutta
Who Should Have a Novice as Attendant

“Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgatena bhikkhunā sāmaṇero upaṭṭhāpetabbo.
“Mendicants, a novice should attend on a mendicant with five qualities.

Katamehi pañcahi?
What five?

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu asekhena sīlakkhandhena samannāgato hoti; asekhena samādhikkhandhena … asekhena paññākkhandhena … asekhena vimuttikkhandhena … asekhena vimuttiñāṇadassanakkhandhena samannāgato hoti.
It’s a mendicant who has the entire spectrum of an adept’s ethics, immersion, wisdom, freedom, and the knowledge and vision of freedom.

Imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgatena bhikkhunā sāmaṇero upaṭṭhāpetabbo”ti.
A novice should attend on a mendicant with these five qualities.”

Tatiyaṁ.

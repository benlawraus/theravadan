Untitled Discourses on Greed

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, pariññāya…
“For the complete understanding of greed …

parikkhayāya…
complete ending …

pahānāya…
giving up …

khayāya…
ending …

vayāya…
vanishing …

virāgāya…
fading away …

nirodhāya…
cessation …

cāgāya…
giving away …

paṭinissaggāya…
letting go …

ime ekādasa dhammā bhāvetabbā”.
these eleven things should be developed.”

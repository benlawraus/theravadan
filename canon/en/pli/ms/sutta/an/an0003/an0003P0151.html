Aṅguttara Nikāya 3.151
Numbered Discourses 3.151

15. Maṅgalavagga
15. Good Fortune

Dutiyakhatasutta
Broken (2nd)

“Tīhi, bhikkhave …pe…
“When a foolish, incompetent untrue person has three qualities they keep themselves broken and damaged. …

sāvajjena kāyakammena, sāvajjena vacīkammena, sāvajjena manokammena …pe….
Blameworthy deeds by way of body, speech, and mind. …

Tīhi, bhikkhave …pe…
When an astute, competent true person has three qualities they keep themselves intact and unscathed. …

anavajjena kāyakammena, anavajjena vacīkammena, anavajjena manokammena …pe….
Blameless deeds by way of body, speech, and mind. …”

Chaṭṭhaṁ.

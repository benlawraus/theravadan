Aṅguttara Nikāya 3.134 Numbered Discourses 3.134

14. Yodhājīvavagga 14. A Warrior

Parisāsutta Assemblies

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, parisā. “Mendicants, there are these three assemblies.

Katamā tisso? What three?

Ukkācitavinītā parisā, paṭipucchāvinītā parisā, yāvatāvinītā parisā—An assembly educated in fancy talk, an assembly educated in questioning, and an assembly educated to the fullest extent.

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso parisā”ti. These are the three assemblies.”

Dutiyaṁ.

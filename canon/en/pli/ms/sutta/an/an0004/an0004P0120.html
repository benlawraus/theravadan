Aṅguttara Nikāya 4.120 Numbered Discourses 4.120

12. Kesivagga 12. With Kesi

Dutiyabhayasutta Perils (2nd)

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, bhayāni. “Mendicants, there are these four perils.

Katamāni cattāri? What four?

Aggibhayaṁ, udakabhayaṁ, rājabhayaṁ, corabhayaṁ—The perils of fire, water, kings, and bandits.

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri bhayānī”ti. These are the four perils.”

Dasamaṁ.

Kesivaggo dutiyo.

Tassuddānaṁ

Kesi javo patodo ca,

Nāgo ṭhānena pañcamaṁ;

Appamādo ca ārakkho,

Saṁvejanīyañca dve bhayāti.

Aṅguttara Nikāya 4.225
Numbered Discourses 4.225

23. Duccaritavagga
23. Bad Conduct

Paṭhamamaggasutta
Path (1st)

…pe… Micchādiṭṭhiko hoti, micchāsaṅkappo hoti, micchāvāco hoti, micchākammanto hoti …pe… sammādiṭṭhiko hoti, sammāsaṅkappo hoti, sammāvāco hoti, sammākammanto hoti …pe….
“A foolish person … creates much wickedness. … wrong view, wrong thought, wrong speech, wrong action. … An astute person … creates much merit. … right view, right thought, right speech, right action. …”

Pañcamaṁ.

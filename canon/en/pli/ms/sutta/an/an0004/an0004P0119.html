Aṅguttara Nikāya 4.119
Numbered Discourses 4.119

12. Kesivagga
12. With Kesi

Paṭhamabhayasutta
Perils (1st)

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, bhayāni.
“Mendicants, there are these four perils.

Katamāni cattāri?
What four?

Jātibhayaṁ, jarābhayaṁ, byādhibhayaṁ, maraṇabhayaṁ—
The perils of rebirth, old age, sickness, and death.

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri bhayānī”ti.
These are the four perils.”

Navamaṁ.

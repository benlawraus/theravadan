Aṅguttara Nikāya 1 Numbered Discourses 1

28. Pasādakaradhammavagga The Chapter on Inspiring Qualities

“Addhamidaṁ, bhikkhave, lābhānaṁ yadidaṁ āraññikattaṁ …pe… “Mendicants, this is definitely something worth having, that is, living in the wilderness …

piṇḍapātikattaṁ … eating only almsfood …

paṁsukūlikattaṁ … wearing rag robes …

tecīvarikattaṁ … having just three robes …

dhammakathikattaṁ … teaching Dhamma …

vinayadharattaṁ … memorizing the monastic law …

bāhusaccaṁ … being very learned …

thāvareyyaṁ … being respected …

ākappasampadā … being well-presented …

parivārasampadā … having a following …

mahāparivāratā … having a large following …

kolaputti … coming from a good family …

vaṇṇapokkharatā … being handsome …

kalyāṇavākkaraṇatā … being one who enunciates well …

appicchatā … having few wishes …

appābādhatā”ti. being healthy.”

(Soḷasa pasādakaradhammā niṭṭhitā.)

Aṅguttara Nikāya 10.100 Numbered Discourses 10.100

10. Upālivagga 10. With Upāli

Abhabbasutta Cannot

“Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ. “Mendicants, without giving up ten things you can’t realize perfection.

Katame dasa? What ten?

Rāgaṁ, dosaṁ, mohaṁ, kodhaṁ, upanāhaṁ, makkhaṁ, paḷāsaṁ, issaṁ, macchariyaṁ, mānaṁ—Greed, hate, delusion, anger, acrimony, disdain, contempt, jealousy, stinginess, and conceit.

ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ. Without giving up these ten things you can’t realize perfection.

Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ. After giving up ten things you can realize perfection.

Katame dasa? What ten?

Rāgaṁ, dosaṁ, mohaṁ, kodhaṁ, upanāhaṁ, makkhaṁ, paḷāsaṁ, issaṁ, macchariyaṁ, mānaṁ—Greed, hate, delusion, anger, acrimony, disdain, contempt, jealousy, stinginess, and conceit.

ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātun”ti. After giving up these ten things you can realize perfection.”

Dasamaṁ.

Upālivaggo pañcamo.

Tassuddānaṁ

Kāmabhogī bhayaṁ diṭṭhi,

vajjiyamāhituttiyā;

Kokanudo āhuneyyo,

thero upāli abhabboti.

Dutiyo paṇṇāsako samatto.

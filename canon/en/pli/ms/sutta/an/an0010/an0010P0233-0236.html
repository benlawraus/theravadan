Aṅguttara Nikāya 10 Numbered Discourses 10.233–236

22. Sāmaññavagga 22. Similarity

~ Untitled Discourses on Ten to Forty Qualities (3rd)

“Dasahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato bālo veditabbo …pe… “A fool is known by ten qualities …

paṇḍito veditabbo …pe… astute person …

vīsatiyā, bhikkhave …pe… twenty …

tiṁsāya, bhikkhave …pe… thirty …

cattārīsāya, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato bālo veditabbo …pe… forty …”

paṇḍito veditabbo …pe…

imehi kho, bhikkhave, cattārīsāya dhammehi samannāgato paṇḍito veditabbo”ti.

Sāmaññavaggo dutiyo.

Aṅguttara Nikāya 6.99 Numbered Discourses 6.99

10. Ānisaṁsavagga 10. Benefit

Dukkhasutta Suffering

“So vata, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kañci saṅkhāraṁ sukhato samanupassanto …pe… “Mendicants, it is quite impossible for a mendicant who regards any condition as pleasurable to accept views that agree with the teaching. …

sabbasaṅkhāre dukkhato samanupassanto …pe… It’s quite possible for a mendicant who regards all conditions as suffering to accept views that agree with the teaching. …”

ṭhānametaṁ vijjati”.

Catutthaṁ.

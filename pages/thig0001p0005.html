Verses of the Senior Nuns 1.5 Verses of the Elder Bhikkhunīs

Ekakanipāta
The Book of the Ones
Chapter of the Ones

Aññatarātissātherīgāthā

Another Tissā Verses of Another Elder Named Tissā

“Tisse yuñjassu dhammehi,
Tissā, apply yourself to good qualities—
“Tissā, bind yourself to the Dhamma;

khaṇo taṁ mā upaccagā;
don’t let the moment pass you by.
waste no time.

Khaṇātītā hi socanti,
For if you miss your moment,
Those who waste time sorrow

nirayamhi samappitā”ti.
you’ll grieve when sent to hell.
when they arrive in hell.”

… Aññatarā tissā therī ….
Another Elder Named Tissā

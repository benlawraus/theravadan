Uttarātherīgāthā

Verses of the Elder Uttarā Uttarā (1st)

“Kāyena saṁvutā āsiṁ,
“I was restrained in body,
I was restrained

vācāya uda cetasā;
speech, and mind.
in body, speech, and mind.

Samūlaṁ taṇhamabbuyha,
Having pulled out craving by the roots,
Having plucked out craving, root and all,

sītibhūtāmhi nibbutā”ti.
I have become cool and quenched.”
I’m cooled and quenched.

… Uttarā therī ….
The Elder Uttarā

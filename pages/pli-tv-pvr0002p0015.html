2.15 Samathavāra

7. The section on settling

Kāyasaṁsaggaṁ sādiyanapaccayā āpattiyo sattannaṁ samathānaṁ katihi samathehi sammanti?
When it comes to the offenses that are a result of consenting to physical contact, through how many of the seven principles for settling legal issues are they settled?

Kāyasaṁsaggaṁ sādiyanapaccayā āpattiyo sattannaṁ samathānaṁ tīhi samathehi sammanti—
Through three of them:

siyā sammukhāvinayena ca paṭiññātakaraṇena ca, siyā sammukhāvinayena ca tiṇavatthārakena ca …pe….
they may be settled by resolution face-to-face and by acting according to what has been admitted; or they may be settled by resolution face-to-face and by covering over as if with grass. …

Dadhiṁ viññāpetvā bhuñjanapaccayā āpattiyo sattannaṁ samathānaṁ katihi samathehi sammanti?
When it comes to the offenses that are a result of asking for curd and then eating it, through how many of the seven principles for settling legal issues are they settled?

Dadhiṁ viññāpetvā bhuñjanapaccayā āpattiyo sattannaṁ samathānaṁ tīhi samathehi sammanti—
Through three of them:

siyā sammukhāvinayena ca paṭiññātakaraṇena ca, siyā sammukhāvinayena ca tiṇavatthārakena ca.
they may be settled by resolution face-to-face and by acting according to what has been admitted; or they may be settled by resolution face-to-face and by covering over as if with grass.

Samathavāro niṭṭhito sattamo.
The seventh section on settling is finished.

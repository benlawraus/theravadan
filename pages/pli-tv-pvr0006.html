6. Khandhakapucchāvāra

6. The section on questioning on the Chapters

Upasampadaṁ pucchissaṁ sanidānaṁ saniddesaṁ,
I will ask about the full ordination, together with its origin story and its detailed explanation:

Samukkaṭṭhapadānaṁ kati āpattiyo;
how many offenses are laid down in the exalted sentences?

Upasampadaṁ vissajjissaṁ sanidānaṁ saniddesaṁ,
I will answer about the full ordination, together with its origin story and its detailed explanation:

Samukkaṭṭhapadānaṁ dve āpattiyo.
two offenses are laid down in the exalted sentences.

Tassuddānaṁ
This is the summary:

Upasampadūposatho,
“Full ordination, observance day,

Vassūpanāyikapavāraṇā;
Entering the rainy-season residence, invitation ceremony;

Cammabhesajjakathinā,
Skins, medicines, robe-making ceremony,

Cīvaraṁ campeyyakena ca.
Robes, and with those from Campā,

Kosambakkhandhakaṁ kammaṁ,
The chapter on Kosambī, legal procedures,

Pārivāsisamuccayā;
Those on probation, the collection;

Samathakhuddakā senā,
Settling of legal issues, short topics, dwllings,

Saṅghabhedaṁ samācāro;
Schism in the Sangha, conduct;

Ṭhapanaṁ bhikkhunikkhandhaṁ,
Cancellation, the chapter on nuns,

Pañcasattasatena cāti.
And with the five hundred, the seven hundred.”

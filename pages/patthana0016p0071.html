16.71. Sanidassanattika, Cittasahabhūduka

Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ cittasahabhuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho nocittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi …pe… avigate cha.

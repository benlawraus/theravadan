Dhammapaccanīyānuloma (4), Tikadukapaṭṭhānapāḷi (4)

22.2. Kusalattika, Sahetukaduka

Nakusalaṁ nasahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca akusalo sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nakusalaṁ nasahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naakusalaṁ nasahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naabyākataṁ nasahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca akusalo sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nakusalaṁ nasahetukañca naabyākataṁ nasahetukañca dhammaṁ paṭicca akusalo sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nakusalaṁ nasahetukañca naakusalaṁ nasahetukañca dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

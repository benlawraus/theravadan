24.19. Saraṇaduka, Cūḷantaraduka

Nasaraṇo nasappaccayo dhammo araṇassa sappaccayassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Ārammaṇe ekaṁ, adhipatiyā upanissaye ekaṁ.

Nasaraṇo nasaṅkhato dhammo. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

Nasaraṇaṁ nasanidassanaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca araṇo sanidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi …pe… vipāke ekaṁ …pe… avigate tīṇi.

Nasaraṇo naanidassano dhammo saraṇassa anidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Nasaraṇo naanidassano dhammo araṇassa anidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṅkhittaṁ.) Ārammaṇe dve, adhipatiyā ekaṁ, upanissaye purejāte atthiyā avigate dve.

Verses of the Senior Monks 1.54

Ekakanipāta
The Book of the Ones

Chaṭṭhavagga
Chapter Six

Uttiyattheragāthā

Uttiya (2nd)

“Vassati devo yathā sugītaṁ,
The sky rains down, like a beautiful song.

Channā me kuṭikā sukhā nivātā;
My little hut is roofed and pleasant, <j>sheltered from the wind.

Tassaṁ viharāmi adutiyo,
I dwell there without a partner:

Atha ce patthayasi pavassa devā”ti.
so rain, sky, if you wish.

… Uttiyo thero ….

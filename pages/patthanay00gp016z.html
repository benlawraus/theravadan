14.34. Niyyānikaduka

Niyyānikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

Aniyyānikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

Niyyānikañca aniyyānikañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

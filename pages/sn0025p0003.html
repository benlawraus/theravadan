Linked Discourses 25.3

1. Cakkhuvagga
1. The Eye

Viññāṇasutta

Consciousness

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.
At Sāvatthī.

“Cakkhuviññāṇaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ vipariṇāmi aññathābhāvi;
“Mendicants, eye consciousness is impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

sotaviññāṇaṁ …
Ear consciousness,

ghānaviññāṇaṁ …
nose consciousness,

jivhāviññāṇaṁ …
tongue consciousness,

kāyaviññāṇaṁ …
body consciousness,

manoviññāṇaṁ aniccaṁ vipariṇāmi aññathābhāvi.
and mind consciousness are impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

Yo bhikkhave …pe…
Someone who has faith and confidence in these principles is called a follower by faith. …”

sambodhiparāyano”ti.

Tatiyaṁ.

17. Hoticanacahotitathāgatosutta

17. A Realized One Both Exists and Doesn’t Exist

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.
At Sāvatthī.

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:
“Mendicants, when what exists, because of grasping what and insisting on what, does the view arise:

‘hoti ca na ca hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?
‘A Realized One both exists and doesn’t exist after death’?” …

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

Sattarasamaṁ.

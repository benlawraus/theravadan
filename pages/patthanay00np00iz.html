Dhammapaccanīyānuloma (4), Tikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (1)

19.15. Ajjhattattikadvaya

Naajjhattaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca bahiddhā dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṁ.

Nabahiddhā dhammaṁ paṭicca ajjhatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṁ. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

Hetuyā dve …pe… avigate dve.

Naajjhattārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

Nabahiddhārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca bahiddhārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

Naajjhattārammaṇañca nabahiddhārammaṇañca dhammaṁ paṭicca ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve. (Saṅkhittaṁ.)

Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā dve …pe… purejāte āsevane dve …pe… avigate cha.

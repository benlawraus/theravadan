Linked Discourses 27

1. Kilesavagga

1. Corruptions

8. Taṇhāsutta

8. Craving

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.
At Sāvatthī.

“Yo, bhikkhave, rūpataṇhāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso.
“Mendicants, desire and greed for craving for sights,

Yo saddataṇhāya …
craving for sounds,

yo gandhataṇhāya …
craving for smells,

yo rasataṇhāya …
craving for tastes,

yo phoṭṭhabbataṇhāya …
craving for touches,

yo dhammataṇhāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso.
or craving for thoughts is a corruption of the mind. …”

Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno …pe…

abhiññā sacchikaraṇīyesu dhammesū”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṁ.

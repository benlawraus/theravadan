Ekakanipāta

Itthivagga

6. Mudulakkhaṇajātaka

“Ekā icchā pure āsi,

aladdhā mudulakkhaṇaṁ;

Yato laddhā aḷārakkhī,

icchā icchaṁ vijāyathā”ti.

Mudulakkhaṇajātakaṁ chaṭṭhaṁ.

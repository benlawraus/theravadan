Khattiyasutta

The Khattiya Aristocrats

“Khattiyo dvipadaṁ seṭṭho,
“The khattiya is the best of bipeds,
“An aristocrat is the best of bipeds,

balībaddo catuppadaṁ;
The ox, the best of quadrupeds;
an ox is the best of quadrupeds,

Komārī seṭṭhā bhariyānaṁ,
A maiden is the best of wives,
a maiden is the best of wives,

yo ca puttāna pubbajo”ti.
The first born, the best of sons. ”
and a first-born the best of sons.”

“Sambuddho dvipadaṁ seṭṭho,
“The Buddha is the best of bipeds,
“A Buddha is the best of bipeds,

ājānīyo catuppadaṁ;
A steed, the best of quadrupeds;
a thoroughbred, the best of quadrupeds,

Sussūsā seṭṭhā bhariyānaṁ,
An obedient woman is the best of wives,
a good listener is the best of wives,

yo ca puttānamassavo”ti.
A dutiful boy, the best of sons. ”
and the best of sons is loyal.”

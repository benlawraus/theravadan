4. Tissātherīgāthā

Verses of the Elder Tissā

Tissā

“Tisse sikkhassu sikkhāya,
“Tissā, train yourself with discipline,
Tissā, train in the trainings—

mā taṁ yogā upaccaguṁ;
do not let bonds tie you down.
don’t let the practice pass you by.

Sabbayogavisaṁyuttā,
All bonds unbound,
Detached from all attachments,

cara loke anāsavā”ti.
walk in the world free from influences.”
live in the world free of defilements.

… Tissā therī ….
The Elder Tissā

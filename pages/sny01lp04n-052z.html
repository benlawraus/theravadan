14. Punagaṅgāpeyyālavagga

14. Another Chapter of Abbreviated Texts on the Ganges, Etc.

131–142. Punagaṅgānadīādisutta

131–142. More on the Ganges River, Etc.

…pe…

vaggo cuddasamo.

Uddānaṁ

Cha pācīnato ninnā,

cha ninnā ca samuddato;

Dvete cha dvādasa honti,

vaggo tena pavuccatīti.

(Bojjhaṅgasaṁyuttassa gaṅgāpeyyālaṁ rāgavasena vitthāretabbaṁ.)
(To be expanded as in SN 45.103–114, removal of greed version.)
